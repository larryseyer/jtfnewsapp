## Description
JTF News delivers verified facts from 22 trusted news sources — with full transparency into how each source is rated for accuracy, bias, speed, and consensus.

Every story card shows you:
• The verified fact
• Source ratings (accuracy & bias scores)
• Who owns each source
• When it was published

Features:
• Daily Digest — Watch or listen to the day's news summary
• Full Archive — Browse and search years of verified facts
• Source Transparency — See accuracy, bias, and ownership for every source
• Watched Terms — Track stories by keyword with automatic notifications
• Bookmarks — Save stories for later reading
• Home Screen Widgets — Small, medium, and large widgets with today's facts
• Live Activities — Lock Screen banner and Dynamic Island when new stories arrive
• Siri Shortcuts — "Hey Siri, what are today's facts?"
• Apple Watch — Standalone watch app with story list and complications
• Share — Polished share text with ratings, ownership, and link
• Offline Support — Read cached stories without internet
• Zero Tracking — No analytics, no accounts, no data collection

JTF News is built on the principle that news should be about facts, not opinions. Every piece of information is cross-referenced across multiple sources and rated for reliability.

Funding is through donations only — no ads, no paywalls, no sponsored content.
